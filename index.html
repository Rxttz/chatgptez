Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter Summaries

Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive summary of all chapters of "Jujutsu Kaisen," a dark fantasy manga by Gege Akutami. Here, you'll find an overview of major story arcs and key events from the series.

Early Arcs

Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical School: Yuji Itadori consumes a powerful cursed object and is thrust into the world of jujutsu sorcery, meeting Satoru Gojo and Sukuna.

The Cursed Womb: Blood Rock: Introduction of new characters like Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki as they start their journey as jujutsu sorcerers.

The Kyoto Sister School Exchange Event

Tokyo Jujutsu High School and Kyoto Jujutsu High School engage in a competition that turns deadly, introducing new rivals and deepening the storyline.

The Shibuya Incident

A major conflict erupts in Shibuya involving curses and chaos. This arc includes significant battles, losses, and revelations.

The Cursed Child Arc

Sukuna's plans unfold further, and new characters and curses are introduced, expanding the lore of the series.

The Dark Tournament

Focuses on intense battles and power struggles within the jujutsu world, showcasing character development and new challenges.

The Current Arc

Details of the ongoing events, including the aftermath of previous arcs and new threats to Yuji and his friends.

Characters

Yuji Itadori: Possesses immense physical strength and resilience. After ingesting a special grade cursed object, he becomes the host of Sukuna, a powerful Curse. Yuji is known for his remarkable endurance and unyielding spirit, fighting against curses to protect others.

Satoru Gojo: A highly skilled jujutsu sorcerer known for his exceptional abilities and the Six Eyes. His Limitless Cursed Technique grants him control over space, making him nearly invincible. Gojo serves as a mentor and protector for his students, pushing them to their limits.

Nobara Kugisaki: Utilizes the Cursed Womb: Blood Rock’s tools in her techniques, such as nails and a hammer. Nobara excels in combat using her unique voodoo-like sorcery to manipulate cursed energy and inflict damage on curses. Her strong-willed nature and courage are central to her character.

Megumi Fushiguro: Master of the Ten Shadows Technique, which allows him to summon Shikigami, powerful creatures that aid him in battle. Megumi is known for his tactical mind and strategic approach to fighting curses. His commitment to protecting others, despite his aloof demeanor, highlights his depth as a character.
